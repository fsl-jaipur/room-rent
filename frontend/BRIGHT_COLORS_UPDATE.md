# Bright Color Scheme Update 🎨

## New Vibrant Color Palette

- **Brand Primary**: `#ff6b35` (Vibrant Orange)
- **Brand Primary Hover**: `#ff5722` (Deep Orange)
- **Brand Accent**: `#ffa726` (Bright Amber)
- **Brand Secondary**: `#ff8a65` (Coral Orange)

### Background & Text
- **Background**: `#fafafa` (Light Gray)

## Where Bright Colors Are Applied

### 1. Gradients
Multiple UI elements now use vibrant gradients:
- **Budget Display**: Orange to amber gradient with white text
- **Checkbox Pills (Selected)**: Orange to amber gradient
- **Badge Overlays**: Orange to amber gradient on listing cards
- **Feature Icons**: Gradient background circles on home page
- **Hover States**: Subtle gradient backgrounds

### 2. Interactive Elements
- **Buttons**: Bright orange primary buttons
- **Links**: Orange active states with gradient backgrounds
- **Focus States**: Orange glow effects (15% opacity)
- **Hover Effects**: Transform + gradient on favorite buttons

### 3. Visual Accents
- **Listing Card Badges**: "2 BHK" badges with gradient + shadow
- **Favorite Button Hover**: Gradient fill with scale animation
- **Filter Header**: Gradient text effect
- **Scrollbar**: Gradient thumb for webkit browsers
- **Navigation Active**: Orange with gradient background

### 4. Shadows & Effects
- **Badge Shadow**: `rgba(255, 107, 53, 0.3)` for depth
- **Focus Ring**: 2px solid orange outline
- **Card Hover**: Enhanced shadows with orange tint

## Design Philosophy

### Energy & Warmth
The bright orange palette creates:
- **Energetic**: Vibrant and eye-catching
- **Warm**: Friendly and inviting
- **Modern**: Contemporary and fresh
- **Confident**: Bold and memorable

### Accessibility
- High contrast maintained for readability
- Focus states clearly visible
- Color not used as sole indicator
- WCAG AA compliant text contrast

### Brand Identity
The orange color scheme:
- Stands out from competitors
- Creates strong brand recognition
- Evokes feelings of enthusiasm and creativity
- Appeals to young, dynamic audience
